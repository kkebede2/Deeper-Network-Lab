🔹 Switch SW1 (Access Layer + Management VLAN)

Role:

- VLAN segmentation
- Trunking to router
- Management VLAN

interface fa0/1
 switchport mode access
 switchport access vlan 10

👉 Assigns end devices to VLANs.

interface fa0/24
 switchport mode trunk
 switchport trunk allowed vlan 10,20,30,99

👉 Carries multiple VLANs to router (router-on-a-stick).

interface vlan 99
 ip address 192.168.99.2 255.255.255.0

👉 Enables remote management of switch.
